For those who do not have their immunization records:

To utilize the Philadelphia Vaccine Forecaster, please start by requesting your Official Immunization Record from the Philadelphia Department of Public Health by clicking the following link: https://redcap.phila.gov/surveys/?s=CJN8APH8JRJHXND8.

Once you receive your record, please return to our website for vaccine timeline recommendations.
