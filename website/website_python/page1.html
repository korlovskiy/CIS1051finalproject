Immunization Page

For those who do not have their immunization records:

To utilize the Philadelphia Vaccine Forecaster, please start by requesting your Official Immunization Record from the Philadelphia Department of Public Health by clicking the following link: https://www.phila.gov/services/mental-physical-health/get-vaccinated/request-your-immunization-record/

*** Once you receive your record, please return to our website for vaccine timeline recommendations.

Upload immunization records:

1) Click the "Choose file" to attach your pdf file.

2) Then, click the "Upload" button to submit the file.

If the upload is successful, you will be directed to the results page.
